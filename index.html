Akira Toriyama (鳥山 明)

Born: April 5, 1955 — Died: March 1, 2024.
Famous works: Dr. Slump, Dragon Ball.
Widely regarded as one of Japan’s most influential manga artists.

Short Biography

Akira Toriyama was a Japanese manga artist and character designer whose work transformed modern shōnen manga. He rose to fame with Dr. Slump (1980–1984) and then Dragon Ball (1984–1995), inspiring generations of artists worldwide.

“His humor, rhythm in action, and unforgettable characters defined an era of manga.”
